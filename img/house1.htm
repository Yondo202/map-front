Comelite Architecture Structure and Interior Design as Architects

Located in Khamis Mushait, Saudi Arabia, this Contemporary Modern House Design by CAS is a brilliant way to evoke a resort-like luxurious vibe in your own home. Extremely simple & modernistic, the artful design of this home perfectly encompasses the cultural & traditional settings of the site context. The wood-glass combo against a backdrop of white makes a bold statement while the gorgeous orthogonally oriented landscape helps emulate the best of nature within the bounds of the property itself.
